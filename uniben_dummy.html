About the Department

Mission • Vision • Values

Our Mission

To cultivate rigorous historical thinking, ethical scholarship, and civic-minded leadership through teaching, research, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A premier center for historical inquiry where tradition and innovation meet to illuminate Africa and the world.

Our Values

Excellence, curiosity, integrity, inclusivity, and service to society.
